Did people know when Fifa started?

According to The first ever Fifa game was released on 15 December 1993. It was pretty ground-breaking, as it used isometric technology, instead of 16-bit style which was popular at the time. The game only included national teams, and real player names were not used.It was pretty famous for a bug that allowed you to score goals by standing in front of a goalkeeper, and running away from referees to avoid getting a card.
